# SynapxIA

**SynapxIA** is a platform designed to support the organized, measurable, and sustainable adoption of Generative Artificial Intelligence within an organization.

The name **SynapxIA** stands for:

> **Services Yielding New Applications and Practices for eXtended AI Adoption**  
> *Servicios Y Nuevas Aplicaciones para la Práctica y eXpansión de la Inteligencia Artificial*

SynapxIA aims to become a central point for coordinating AI initiatives, classifying and reusing digital assets, managing teams, measuring adoption levels, analyzing generated impact, and connecting AI opportunities with the organization's business processes.

The platform is not limited to being an asset repository or an initiative registration system. Its purpose is to articulate an AI adoption ecosystem where people, teams, initiatives, assets, processes, and indicators coexist in an integrated way.

---

## Purpose

The main purpose of SynapxIA is to help the organization:

- Identify opportunities for using Generative AI.
- Register, evaluate, and prioritize AI initiatives.
- Organize teams, roles, and responsibilities related to AI adoption.
- Build and maintain a library of reusable assets.
- Define a common taxonomy for classifying digital and AI assets.
- Measure adoption progress by teams, units, roles, and dimensions.
- Analyze the impact generated by AI on processes, productivity, quality, costs, user experience, and decision-making.
- Connect initiatives, assets, and processes to promote scalable and governed adoption.

---

## Functional Scope

SynapxIA is organized into functional modules that cover different aspects of the AI adoption lifecycle:

1. 🛠️ **Administration**  
   Base platform configuration, including users, profiles, modules, options, lists, and privileges.

2. 🏷️ **Asset Taxonomy**  
   Definition of categories, features, and specifications to classify digital and AI assets.

3. 👥 **Collaboration**  
   Management of teams, collaborative roles, assignments, projects, dimensions, and adoption metrics.

4. 📚 **Asset Library**  
   Repository of reusable assets such as prompts, MCPs, agents, flows, skills, assistants, RAG apps, and models.

5. 🚀 **Initiatives**  
   Registration, evaluation, prioritization, diagnosis, and tracking of AI initiatives.

6. 📊 **Analytics**  
   Dashboards, reports, parameters, executions, favorites, and permissions for analyzing usage, adoption, and impact.

7. 🔄 **Processes**  
   Representation of value chains, process maps, and process models to connect AI adoption with organizational operations.

---

## Functional Principles

SynapxIA is based on the following functional principles.

### 🧭 Governance and Traceability

Every relevant element of the platform must be manageable, searchable, and auditable.

This includes users, profiles, assets, initiatives, projects, dashboards, permissions, actions, collaborations, and metrics. The goal is to ensure that AI adoption can be governed with visibility, accountability, and historical traceability.

### ♻️ Knowledge Reuse

The assets generated during AI adoption should be reusable.

SynapxIA seeks to prevent prompts, agents, MCPs, assistants, flows, models, and other resources from remaining scattered across teams or tools. Instead, the platform organizes them in a shared library so they can be discovered, improved, governed, and reused.

### 📏 Measurable Adoption

AI adoption must be measured in a structured way.

SynapxIA supports the definition of dimensions, scales, metrics, and dashboards to observe progress by teams, roles, units, and other organizational criteria. This allows the organization to understand not only whether AI is being used, but also how, where, and with what level of maturity.

### 🤝 Organizational Collaboration

AI adoption does not depend only on technology.

It requires teams, roles, responsibilities, projects, assignments, and collaboration spaces. SynapxIA incorporates these structures to coordinate the work of people and areas involved in the adoption process.

### 🔗 Connection with Processes

AI initiatives and assets must be related to real organizational processes.

The Processes module helps identify where AI can improve activities, decisions, time, quality, experience, or efficiency. This ensures that AI adoption is connected to operational needs and business value instead of being managed as isolated experiments.

---

## Expected Value

SynapxIA helps organizations move from isolated AI experiments to a governed adoption model where:

- Opportunities are registered and evaluated.
- Initiatives are prioritized and monitored.
- Reusable assets are created and evolved.
- Teams and responsibilities are clearly organized.
- Adoption and impact are measured.
- AI efforts are connected to real business processes.

Its main value is to articulate people, knowledge, assets, processes, and indicators into a structured, traceable, and scalable practice for Generative AI adoption.
